Official League Rules

By participating in the pool you agree to the following:

THE BASICS

  • Every team will select one golfer for each scheduled tournament
  • Once you have selected a golfer you cannot select them again
  • Player selections must be inputted before the first golfer tees off at each tournament
  • The selected player’s weekly earnings will contribute to the cumulative earnings for each team in the pool
  • Teams will be ranked in order of highest cumulative earnings to lowest cumulative earnings

    • ENTRY FEE

  • CAD $50
  • Email entry fee to davekyle@gmail.com before the Farmers Insurance Open. If entry fees are not received by AT&T Pebble Beach Pro Am, participant will be removed from the pool.

    • PAYOUT STRUCTURE

  • 1st place = 70% total pot
  • 2nd place = 20% total pot
  • 3rd place = 10% total pot

    • “STRIKE” RULE

    Each team has one “strike” at the beginning of the season. A “strike” means if a pool participant fails to make their selection before the first player tees off in the tournament, the participant is able to select a golfer from those yet to tee off. You have the ability to do this once per season..

    POSTPONED OR CANCELLED EVENTS

    If a tournament is postponed or cancelled for all players during its scheduled time, all OAD participants will receive their selections back. In other words, you have the ability to pick them at a later tournament. Call it “the Players rule”. If the cancelled or postponed event is rescheduled for AFTER The Northern Trust tournament, it will not count in this pool’s results.

    ALTERNATE GOLFER SELECTION

    To account for golfer withdrawals, you can select an “Alternate” golfer. This player will be used in the event that your primary selection withdraws from the tournament.

    GOLFER WITHDRAWALS

    If your golfer withdraws after they have teed off in the tournament, your alternate selection will become your primary pick.
